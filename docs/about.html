About Pour Picks

A bourbon collector's cellar journal — built and run by one person.

Quick Answer

Pour Picks is an iOS journal and cellar catalog for adult bourbon collectors, built and operated by an independent developer. The app does not sell alcohol, show alcohol ads, or encourage consumption. It is a private reference for tracking what you own, what you pour, and what your collection is worth.

Who runs Pour Picks?

Pour Picks is built and operated by an independent solo developer and published by Timberline Ventures LLC, the developer's own company. There is no outside investor and no advertising network behind the app. Decisions about features, privacy, and direction are made by one person, and that person answers the email at support@pourpicks.app.

What is Pour Picks?

Pour Picks is an iOS app that does four things for adult bourbon collectors: catalogs the contents of your cellar, scans bottles to identify what you own, tracks the value of your collection over time, and gives you a private journal for every pour. The reference catalog covers thousands of American whiskeys and grows over time, corrected by users when something is missing or wrong.

What Pour Picks is not

  • It is not a retailer. The app does not sell, distribute, or deliver alcohol.
  • It is not a marketplace. There are no listings, no buy/sell flows, no auctions, no allocation tools.
  • It does not show alcohol ads. There are no ads of any kind in the app.
  • It is not a recommendation engine to drink more. The app exists to help people who already collect bourbon keep their cellar organized.
  • It does not collect health, location, or financial data. See the privacy policy for the full breakdown.

How Pour Picks makes money

The core app is free. A Cask Club subscription unlocks advanced collection tracking and journaling features. That is the entire revenue model. Subscriptions are managed through Apple, so payment information never touches Pour Picks' servers.

Editorial standards

Articles published on this site are written in-house, based on first-hand experience as a collector and on cited primary sources (distillery technical sheets, industry reference books, TTB filings, and other public sources). When a fact comes from somewhere other than direct experience, it is linked. When something is an opinion, it is labeled.

Articles are dated, and the date is updated only when the content actually changes — not just to make the page look fresh.

Responsible-use notice

Pour Picks does not promote alcohol consumption. Excessive consumption of alcoholic beverages is harmful to health, and impaired driving is illegal everywhere we operate. The app's full responsibility disclaimer lives in the Terms of Use.
