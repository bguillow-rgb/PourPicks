Quick Answer

Pour Picks is a hobby and lifestyle iOS app for adult bourbon collectors. It catalogs your cellar, scans bottles to identify what you own, tracks the value of your collection over time, and gives you a private journal to log every pour. It does not sell alcohol.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pour Picks?

Pour Picks is an iOS journal app for adult bourbon collectors. You catalog your cellar, scan bottles to identify them, track collection value, and log tasting notes, all in one private reference.

Is Pour Picks a retailer?

No. Pour Picks does not sell alcohol, distribute alcohol, or encourage consumption. It is a reference, journal, and cellar catalog for collectors of legal drinking age.

How old do I have to be to use Pour Picks?

Pour Picks is intended for adults of legal drinking age (21+ in the United States). A 21+ age gate is enforced on first launch.

How does the bottle scanner work?

Point the camera at the front label. The Pour Concierge scanner uses Claude Vision to identify the distillery, expression, and proof, then matches against our catalog of thousands of American whiskeys. If recognition fails you can describe the bottle in words, search manually, or suggest a bottle to be added.

Is Pour Picks free?

Yes, the core app is free. A Pro subscription (Cask Club) unlocks advanced collection tracking, the full Pour Concierge scanner, tasting reviews, and pairing recommendations. Pro is managed through Apple subscriptions and can be canceled any time in iOS Settings.

Is my collection data private?

Yes. Your cellar, tasting reviews, scan history, and ratings are tied to your account and never shared, sold, or used for advertising. See the Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.

Will Pour Picks be available on Android?

Pour Picks launched on iOS first. An Android release is on the roadmap but not yet scheduled.
