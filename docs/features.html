Pour Picks

Features

Everything Pour Picks does, in one place.

Your cellar, organized

Track every bottle by distillery, expression, proof, age, and fill level. Mark bottles Owned, Wishlist, or Finished, and capture your own ratings the moment you set the glass down.

Filter by status, search by name, and see at a glance what's open, what's still sealed, and what's earned a place on the wishlist.

A catalog built for discovery

Browse a curated database of thousands of American whiskeys. Filter by category (bourbon, rye, Tennessee, wheat, single-malt American), distillery, age, proof, and price tier — or open the world-spirits scope when you want to look beyond.

Every entry is curated with mash bill, flavor tags, food and drink pairings, and a path back to your cellar.

Pour Concierge: scan any bottle

Point your camera at the front label. The Pour Concierge uses Claude Vision to identify the distillery, expression, and proof, then matches against the catalog — usually in under a second.

Best-effort by design: if the read isn't right, describe the bottle in words for a second pass, search manually, or suggest a new bottle for the catalog. The undo snackbar gives you 4.5 seconds to reverse any cellar add.

Find your next pour

The basic three-question quiz matches your palate to the right catalog entries. The advanced nine-question quiz goes deep with adventure-mode reranking — "Stick to Classics" boosts the icons, "Surprise Me" pulls forward deep cuts.

A tasting journal that remembers

Log every pour with overall rating, separate nose / palate / finish ratings, served-with (neat, rocks, water, cocktail), pour size, and freeform notes. Edit and revisit any entry later.

Public aggregate rating gets shown on the bottle detail page so other collectors can decide whether to chase it.

Collection value at a glance

Cellar value updates with every add: MSRP from the catalog as the default, with overrides for the price you actually paid. Per-bottle and per-line totals, with quantity steppers for the bottles you have multiples of.
