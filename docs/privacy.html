Pour Picks

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2026

Pour Picks ("we", "our", or "the app") is a whiskey and spirits recommendation, identification, and personal cellar utility. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo as provided by the identity provider. Guest users are assigned an anonymous identifier with no personal information collected.

User-generated content. Bottle photos you scan, cellar entries, tasting notes, ratings, reviews, and quiz responses are stored in your account.

Device information. We collect basic device metadata (OS version, device model) for crash reporting and compatibility, plus a durable device identifier used to enforce free-scan quotas. We do not collect location data.

Purchase information. Subscription transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive a purchase confirmation but never see your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide bottle identification, recommendations, and personalized results.
  • To sync your cellar, tasting reviews, and scan history across devices.
  • To improve our identification accuracy using anonymized scan data.
  • To manage your subscription and account.
  • To detect and prevent abuse (rate limits on AI features).

3. Bottle Scan Images

Photos you take with the Pour Concierge feature are uploaded to our servers for identification and forwarded to Anthropic for processing. These images may be retained in anonymized form to improve our identification models. Images are associated with your user ID for correction and history purposes but are not shared publicly.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Supabase \u2014 database and authentication hosting.
  • Anthropic \u2014 AI-powered bottle identification, receives scan images only.
  • RevenueCat \u2014 subscription management.
  • Apple \u2014 authentication and payment processing.
  • Google \u2014 authentication, when used.
  • Sentry \u2014 crash reporting.
  • PostHog \u2014 privacy-respecting product analytics.

Community ratings and reviews you submit are visible to other users. Your display name is shown alongside reviews; your email is never displayed.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which permanently removes all associated data including cellar entries, tasting reviews, scan history, and submitted bottles.

6. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in secured, access-controlled databases. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device. We follow industry-standard practices to protect your information.

7. Age Gate & Children's Privacy

Pour Picks is intended exclusively for adults of legal drinking age (21+ in the United States). The app presents an age confirmation on first launch and blocks users who decline. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21. If we learn that a user is under the legal drinking age, we will delete their account and associated data.

8. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To delete your account and all associated data:

  • In-app: open the Profile tab and tap "Delete Account" at the bottom.
  • By email: if you cannot access the app, email support@pourpicks.app from your account email with the subject "Delete My Account". We will permanently remove your account within 7 days.

California residents and EU users have additional rights under CCPA and GDPR respectively, including the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell), the right to know what data we hold, and the right to be forgotten.
